Zhaoyu Zhang (张兆裕)

I am a Researcher in Huawei 2012 Labs, Darwin Research Center, London, UK.

I obtained my Ph.D. under the supervision of Dr. Yang Hua, Dr. Sean McLoone and Dr Hui Wang in Queen’s University Belfast (QUB), UK, 2025. My Ph.D. research was fully funded by the DfE scholarship. I obtained my Master’s and Bachlor’s degrees from Univeristy of Science and Techonolgy of China, China, advised by Prof. Jun Yu, on 2020 and 2017, respectively.

I have more than eight years of combined research and engineering experience in computer vision and machine learning, focusing on fundamental research topics and real-world applications, with a particular emphasis on:

  • Generitive Models
  • Data-Efficient Learning
  • Reasoning of Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Efficient Hardware Design
